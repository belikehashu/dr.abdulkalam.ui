About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (born Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam) was a prominent Indian scientist, aerospace engineer, and the 11th President of India, serving from 2002 to 2007. He is widely known as the "Missile Man of India" for his crucial role in India's missile technology program. Kalam was also a key figure in India's civilian space program, particularly with the development of the Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III). and a lot he done
